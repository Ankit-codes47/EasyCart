Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about EasyCart.

How do I place an order?

Visit the Products page, choose the products you want and add them to your shopping cart.

How can I track my order?

Visit the Track Order page and enter your EasyCart order ID and registered mobile number.

Can I save my delivery address?

Yes. Use the Address page to enter and save your delivery information.

What happens if a product is unavailable?

An unavailable product can be marked as out of stock or replaced according to customer preference.

Does EasyCart provide home delivery?

EasyCart can provide home delivery according to the available service locations.

How can I contact customer support?

Visit the Contact Us page to send your questions about orders, products or deliveries.

Can I submit a customer review?

Yes. Visit the Customer Reviews page and submit your rating and feedback.

How do I create an EasyCart account?

Visit the Sign Up page and enter your name, mobile number, email and password.
